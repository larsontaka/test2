What is Mediation? ,

Mediation is a process in which two parties attempt to resolve or manage a conflict.
Mediation is unique in which a judge does not constitute a decision; the parties do!
Mediators to create a resolution that is beneficial for both parties that is legally binded through the court system. It is cheaper and provides a unique opportunity to find nonstandard agreements that are not typically provided by a judge.
Mediation saves time and money for all parties involved. .
